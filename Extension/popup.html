I CAN'T SELECT A CURSOR! / MY CURSOR ISN'T SHOWING UP!

The cursor menu now auto-saves. To select a cursor, click the button below the cursor that you want, and your choice will automatically save. You can test your new cursor in the options menu, but it will not appear in your webpage straight away. Once you have selected your cursor, it is important that you refresh any tabs that you have open. Your new cursor will not appear until you have refreshed the tab. You only need to refresh tabs when changing the cursor, after that it will load automatically.

THE CURSOR IS NOT WORKING ON SOME SITES / TOOLBARS

Chrome extensions can do many things, including running code. This makes them a good way to hack users. To stop this from happening, chrome extensions are not allowed to work on anything that is not the webpage. This includes:

  • The bookmarks bar
  • The URL bar
  • The tabs bar and the window controls (minimize, maximize, close)
  • The main scroll bar

Most importantly​, the extension will not work on the Chrome Web Store or most of the chrome generated pages. Chrome generated pages are pages that begin with "chrome://" and contain important and private information such as your history or your settings.
